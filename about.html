Welcome to American Dream Hub, your go-to resource for international students looking to study in the United States and for US residents seeking permanent residency. Our mission is to help you navigate the often complex and overwhelming processes of college admissions and visa processing, so you can focus on achieving your academic and career goals.

We understand that studying in the US is a dream for many students, but it can be challenging to navigate the admission process, including the application requirements, deadlines, and standardized tests like the SAT or GRE. Our team of experts has years of experience in assisting international students, and we are here to guide you every step of the way. From finding the right program to preparing your application materials, we are committed to helping you succeed.

For US residents seeking permanent residency, we know that the process can be daunting. With multiple employment pathways to choose from, including EB3, EB2, H1B, and non-employment pathways such as family-sponsored and marriage-based, it can be challenging to know which option is right for you. At American Dream Hub, we provide comprehensive guidance on each pathway, helping you understand the eligibility requirements, prepare your application materials , and navigate the process with confidence.

At American Dream Hub, we are committed to providing you with personalized assistance and support. Our team of experts has a deep understanding of the US higher education system and the visa application process, and we are here to help you make the most of your academic journey.
